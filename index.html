Game context

Gloria Johnson, the most famous diamond thief in the world, has pulled off the heist of the most valuable diamond ever known: the "Ocean" diamond. After her capture, detectives from the main intelligence agencies rushed to interrogate her, seeking the glory of finding the prized "Ocean" diamond. But Gloria, always cunning, is not willing to reveal her secret so easily. She has proposed a challenge: she will only answer questions if the detectives are willing to participate in her game.

Which detective will be clever enough to discover the whereabouts of the "Ocean" diamond?

Objective

Discover in which European country the "Ocean" diamond is hidden using your deductive skills. Players must ask strategic questions to get closer and closer to the diamond's whereabouts.

In which country will be hidden?

Choose wisely!

In this game, each card presents a question that brings you closer to the ultimate goal, but success isn’t just about luck: here, you’ll need to strategically plan each answer to tackle challenges and outsmart your opponents. As you progress, your skill in choosing the right question will be key to victory.

Fun, strategy, and learning—one card at a time!
